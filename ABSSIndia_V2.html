of UNESCO — Culture Division

Core Programmes

Three Pillars of Cultural Excellence

Each programme is designed to identify raw talent, provide world-class platforms, and build bridges between India and the global arts community.

🏆
Pillar 01
National Art Talent Search

India's most prestigious cross-disciplinary arts competition spanning all 28 states — discovering the next generation of classical performers since 1992.

Explore NATS →
🎭
Pillar 02
Cultural Olympiad (COPA)

An international multi-art festival hosted in Dubai, Singapore and Monaco — where Indian artists compete and collaborate on the world stage.

Explore COPA →
🌐
Pillar 03
Global Council of Art & Culture

An 18-nation advisory council that shapes policy, facilitates exchanges, and ensures ABSS remains a living bridge between civilisations.

Meet the Council →
